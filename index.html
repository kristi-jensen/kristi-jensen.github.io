Chromosome Dynamics

Our lab studies various aspects of chromosome biology ranging from the maintenance of chromosome ends by telomerase to the effects of hybridization, ploidy changes and unisexual reproduction in the context of evolution.

Research Topics

Telomeres & Telomerase

We study telomerase biogenesis with a particular interest in the processing of the ncRNA subunit and the mechanisms that regulate the amount of telomerase activity in the cell.

Publications Learn More

Meiosis & Reproduction

We are interested in the cellular and molecular mechanisms that trigger a switch to parthenogenesis (virgin birth) and permit females of some species to reproduce clonally over many generations.

Publications Learn More

Hybridization & Ploidy

Lizards of the genus Aspidoscelis provide a fascinating system to examine the effects of hybridization and ploidy changes on gene expression, development and fitness.

Publications Learn more
